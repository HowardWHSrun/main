Research & Projects

Recent undergraduate research experiences

Computational Biology

HIV T Cell Dynamics Modeling

University of Delaware - Prof. Ryan Zurakowski

Worked on developing a PDE-based spatial model to study CD4+ T cell and HIV reservoir dynamics under antiretroviral therapy. Used MATLAB to analyze steady-state behavior and create visualizations of treatment outcomes.

MATLAB PDE Modeling Spatial Analysis Computational Biology
Neuroscience & ML

EEG Frequency Domain Analysis

Swarthmore College - Prof. Moser & Prof. Zinszer

Applied Fast Fourier Transform to EEG data from infants and adults during object recognition tasks. Used SVM classification to analyze patterns and studied coherence across electrodes to examine developmental differences in visual processing.

Code Poster
Python SVM Signal Processing EEG Analysis
Biomechanics

Thumb Kinematics Motion Capture

Swarthmore College - Prof. Towles

Helped develop an opto-mechanical system to measure 3D thumb-tip trajectories in cadaveric specimens. Assisted with calibrating the motion capture system and analyzing variation in tip displacement across thumb muscles.

NCUR Poster
