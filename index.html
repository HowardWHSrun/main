CURRENT RESEARCH · 2024–PRESENT
Computational Immunology

Spatial Antigen-Driven T-Cell Dynamics

University of Delaware - Prof. Ryan Zurakowski

Developed a finite-volume model on a reconstructed 3D murine lymph-node mesh to study how spatial antigen exposure shapes activated and memory T-cell dynamics. The current implementation treats antigen as a transported state concentrated near high endothelial venules and calibrates the response against longitudinal LCMV data.

12fitted parameters
16LCMV time points
200bootstrap fits
BMES Poster
MATLAB Finite Volume 3D Meshes Uncertainty Quantification

Robotics, RL & Embedded Systems

WALTER: Bio-Inspired Hexapod Locomotion

Swarthmore Engineering - with Matthew Shiley; advisor Allan Moser

Co-designed and fabricated WALTER (Walking Alternating Tripod for Evolutionary Research), a 12-DOF hexapod with CAD-designed parts, custom Hexapod V2 PCBs, Teensy control, and inertial sensing. Built optimized alternating-tripod gait workflows, deterministic MuJoCo terrain replay, and PPO training and evaluation with archived checkpoints and videos.

Multi-Camera Macaque Behavior Analysis

Ruten Inc. - Brain-Computer Interface Research

Built analysis and validation tools for reconstructing macaque jaw motion from synchronized high-speed camera views. Extended the tracking pipeline with 3D trajectory quality control, CT-referenced geometry, chewing-sidedness analysis, and gape/feeding evaluation so behavioral conclusions are tied to measurable tracking quality.

Neuroscience & ML

EEG Frequency Domain Analysis

Swarthmore College - Prof. Moser & Prof. Zinszer

Applied Fast Fourier Transform to EEG data from infants and adults during object recognition tasks. Used SVM classification to analyze patterns and studied coherence across electrodes to examine developmental differences in visual processing.

Code Poster

Thumb Kinematics Motion Capture

Swarthmore College - Prof. Towles

Helped develop an opto-mechanical system to measure 3D thumb-tip trajectories in cadaveric specimens. Assisted with calibrating the motion capture system and analyzing variation in tip displacement across thumb muscles.
