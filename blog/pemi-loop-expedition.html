Moreover , doing the Pemi Loop offers several advantages: ● The bragging rights of conquering'a challenging trail ● A shorter car commute compared to the Presidential Traverse ● Less time spent in the wilderness compared to'a multi-day trip Technical Considerations (Why We Can Do It) To gauge the difficulty and estimate time, I use'a simple rule: take the Fastest Known Time (FKT) for the route and multiply it by three for normal hikers.

For example, the FKT for Rim-to-Rim-to-Rim in the Grand Canyon is about'6 hours. For'a good hiker , that would translate to roughly 18 hours, which matches my experience, having completed it twice with non-runners under harsh conditions around 18 to 20 hours.

Several sources, including Trekking Sketches and Reddit , suggest'a reasonable time for an experienced hiker to complete the Pemi Loop is around 15-18 hours. This time frame presents'a challenging but achievable goal.
